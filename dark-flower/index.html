Pacific Rhododendron

Rhododendron: The State Flower of Washington

Name of plant: Pacific Rhododendron

Common alternate name of the plant: Coast Rhododendron, California Rhododendron, California Rosebay

Range of physical size: Typically 3-15 feet tall but can reach up to 26 feet.

Geographic Range: West of the Cascades from southern Colombia to Northern California

Best Climate Types: Temperate maritime Pacific Northwest climates. Thriving in well-draining and acidic soils as an understory shrub in conifer forests.
