Rice Crispy Treats

Halloween themed classic childhood sweet treat.

Halloween Rice Crispy Treats Ingredients

  • 8oz Jet-Puffed Marshmallows
  • 16oz Kellogg's Rice Krispies® cereal
  • Orange Food Coloring
  • Stick of butter

Directions

  1. In large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
  2. Add KELLOGG'S® RICE KRISPIES® cereal. Stir until well coated.
  3. Add 2-3 drops food coloring and mix thoroughly.
  4. Using buttered spatula or wax paper evenly press mixture into 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan coated with cooking spray. Cool.
    Cut into 2-inch squares. Best if served the same day.
