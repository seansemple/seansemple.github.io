About Me

Hi, I’m Sean. I cook every day and have been collecting recipes for years. In that time, I’ve followed a lot of amazing recipes – and more than a few truly bad ones. After enough disappointing dinners, I’ve gotten really good at spotting which recipes are worth your time.

This site is my personal collection of “best of the best” dishes, organized by meal type: appetizers, entrees, and desserts. Use the drop-down menu at the top of the page to jump straight to what you’re craving.

Every recipe page includes:

  • A large photo of the finished dish
  • Clear ingredient lists
  • Step-by-step directions
  • Yield, prep time, and cook time

My goal is simple: make it easy and pleasant for you to find something delicious, cook it confidently, and actually want to make it again.
