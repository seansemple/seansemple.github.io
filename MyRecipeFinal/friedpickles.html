Fried Pickles

Crunchy, tangy dill pickle chips in a crisp seasoned coating.

Yield: ~4 servings | Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 5 minutes

Fried Pickles Ingredients

  • 3–4 large kosher dill pickles, sliced into ¼-inch rounds
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Neutral oil for frying (vegetable, canola, or peanut)

Directions

  1. Heat about 1 inch of oil in a heavy pot to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Pat pickle slices dry. Whisk buttermilk and egg in one bowl; mix flour, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne in another.
  3. Dredge pickles in flour, dip in buttermilk mixture, then coat again in flour, shaking off excess.
  4. Fry in batches for 2–3 minutes, until golden and crisp. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Serve warm with ranch or your favorite dipping sauce.
