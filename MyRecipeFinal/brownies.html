Fudgy Brownies

Rich, fudgy brownies with a shiny crackly top.

Yield: 1 (9×9-inch) pan, about 16 brownies | Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 28–30 minutes

Brownie Ingredients

  • 1 cup (120 g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup (43 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon espresso powder (optional)
  • ¾ cup (170 g) unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons (28 ml) oil (canola, vegetable, or coconut)
  • 1⅓ cups (265 g) granulated sugar, divided
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract (optional)
  • ¾ cup (128 g) chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 9×9-inch pan with parchment and lightly grease.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, cocoa powder, and espresso powder in a large bowl.
  3. In a saucepan, melt butter with ⅓ cup sugar and the oil over medium heat, stirring until combined. Remove from heat.
  4. In a mixing bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolk, vanilla, and remaining sugar. Slowly whisk in the warm butter mixture.
  5. Fold in dry ingredients and chocolate chips just until no dry streaks remain.
  6. Spread batter into the pan and bake 28–30 minutes, until edges are set and the top is shiny and slightly cracked. Cool completely before slicing.
