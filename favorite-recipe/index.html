Muhammara

Flavorful walnut dip with a spicy kick!

Muhammara Ingredients

  • 3 roasted red bell peppers
  • ½ cup bread crumbs
  • ½ cup walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1½ tablespoons pomegranate molasses
  • 1 teaspoon aleppo pepper flakes
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • fresh mint for garnish
  • pita bread for serving

Directions

  1. In a blender or food processor, add red peppers, breadcrumbs, olive oil, walnuts, garlic, pomegranate molasses,
    lemon juice, aleppo pepper, cumin and a generous amount of ground pepper.
    Important: Do not over-blend. It's important to pulse until creamy and then season to taste.
  2. Garnish your dip with fresh mint or additional pomegranate molasses. Best served with fresh pita bread!
