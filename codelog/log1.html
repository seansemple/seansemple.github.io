What is the difference between non-semantic and semantic tags in HTML?

Semantic elements such as <header>, <nav>, <article>, <section> and <footer>, describe the role and structure of the content, improving accessibility and search-engine-optimization. Non-semantic elements, such as <div> and <span> are used primarily for structural grouping.

What are two (or more) benefits of using semantic tags?

It may improve the webpage's ranking when it's being searched for. Additionally, it is easier for persons with diabilities to use your website.
